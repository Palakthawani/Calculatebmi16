LET US KNOW WHAT IS BMI AND HOW TO CALCULATE IT-


BMI refers to Body Mass Index. Body mass index (BMI) is a tool that healthcare providers use to
estimate the amount of body fat by using your height and weight measurements. It can help assess
risk factors for certain health conditions.It is used to classify whether a person is underweight,normal weight,overweight or obese.

THE FORMULA TO CALCULATE BMI:-
BMI=Weight(kg)/Height(m)2
